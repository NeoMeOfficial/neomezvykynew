# Reference visuals

The PNG snapshots aren't shipped flat in this folder. Open the **live prototype** instead — it's higher fidelity and lets you scrub between variants:

1. Open `neome/NeoMe Final Review.html` in the project.
2. Find the section header for the screen you're building (e.g. "03 · Domov (Home) — CANONICAL").
3. Hover any artboard and click the **↗ expand** icon at the start of the label row to open it fullscreen.
4. Use ← / → to step through variants in that section.

For each trial-batch screen, the artboard IDs are listed in its `PROMPT.md`.

If you want flat PNGs anyway:

1. Open the file in your browser at 100% zoom.
2. Right-click the artboard → "Save image as…" or use a screenshot tool.
3. macOS: ⌘⇧4 then space, click the artboard.

The prototype is the source of truth. The screenshots are just for your design review meetings.
